# Another fitness app Constitution

## Core Principles

### Performance First
The application must prioritize speed and efficiency. Services must load quickly (under 4 seconds) to ensure a seamless experience. All performance tests must be successfully passed during the testing phase.

### User-Centric Design
Visuals and navigation must be intuitive, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing. The design should align with the health and exercise theme, ensuring that users can easily track their habits and progress.

### High Availability and Stability
The project is considered a success when it is available at all times without constant crashes. Robustness and reliability are non-negotiable to maintain user trust.

### Data Continuity and Accessibility
Users must have the ability to consult their latest data at any time. This includes access to informative graphs that visualize their athletic and dietary progress over time.

### Effective and Safe Personalization
The application must deliver personalized fitness routines and nutritional recommendations tailored to the user's goals and profile. These suggestions must be both effective and medically safe.

## Scope and Exclusions

### Target Audience
The project is designed for the general public between 18 and 35 years old, specifically targeting those interested in sports and healthy living habits.

### MVP Exclusions
The initial version (MVP) of Another fitness app does not include:
- A 100% guarantee of results (as success depends on user adherence).
- Real-time calorie measuring or food photography.
- Capability to download results.
- More than 5 defined use cases for sports and eating habits.
- Download PDFs.
- Share content with other users or social netwroks.


## Success Definition

### Impact Areas
The application aims to have a positive impact on overall health, with a specific focus on structuring athletic and dietary habits based on the user's profile.

### Success Metrics
Success is achieved when:
- Users receive personalized and safe routines/recommendations according to their profile and biometric parameters.
- Progress is clearly visualized through graphs.
- The system remains stable, fast, and accessible under user load.
- Give right data, correct and precise suggestions about nutrittion and exercises based on user profile and validated resources.
- User information is stored securely and confidentially.

## Governance
This constitution supersedes all other practices within the project. Amendments require thorough documentation, version incrementing, and a migration plan for any dependent artifacts. All Pull Requests and reviews must verify compliance with these core principles.
